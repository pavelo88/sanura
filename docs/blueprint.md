# **App Name**: SANURA Aesthetics

## Core Features:

- Treatment Catalog Display: Showcase a categorized portfolio of aesthetic and plastic surgery treatments with detailed descriptions and operational information.
- Patient Testimonial Showcase: Display patient success stories and testimonials, including ratings, to build trust and social proof.
- WhatsApp Lead Generation Form: Capture user interest through an interactive form and directly initiate a WhatsApp conversation with pre-filled details for booking consultations. Uses 'https://wa.me/593984246986'.
- AI-Powered Treatment Guide: An AI-powered tool that uses reasoning to help users identify potential treatments tailored to their aesthetic goals and physical characteristics based on a guided questionnaire.
- Dynamic Theme Switching: Allow users to toggle between a light and dark interface theme, preserving their preference across sessions.
- Responsive Navigation Menu: Provide a clear and accessible navigation menu that adapts to different screen sizes, including a mobile-friendly toggle.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light Theme Primary: A confident Teal, representing trust and professionalism. Converted from HSL(175, 83%, 32%) to RGB hex: '#0D9488'. blanco y cyan
- Light Theme Background: A crisp, nearly white with a subtle teal undertone, ensuring a clean and modern feel. Converted from HSL(175, 20%, 95%) to RGB hex: '#E8F8F7'. blanco y cyan
- Light Theme Accent: A subtle yet distinctive Dusty Mauve (#D794AA), for secondary highlights and interactive elements, mirroring the dark theme's secondary.
- Dark Theme Primary: A striking Vibrant Magenta (#E30B8D), commanding attention for headlines and key interactive components, embodying modern luxury.
- Dark Theme Background: A deep, immersive Ebony with a subtle Plum Undertone (#1A0815), setting a dramatic and sophisticated mood.
- Dark Theme Accent: A luminous Soft Rose Gold (#D794AA), used for secondary text and subtle accents, adding a touch of understated glamour.
- Headlines and prominent titles will use 'Alegreya' (serif) for an elegant, intellectual, and contemporary feel. Body text will utilize 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective readability and neutrality across various content types.
- Utilize geometric, outline-style icons from the Lucide.dev library (e.g., ShieldCheck, HeartPulse, MessageCircle) to maintain a consistent, modern, and clean visual language. Icons emphasize themes of trust, health, and communication.
- A responsive, content-focused layout with generous spacing and a grid-based system for presenting treatments and testimonials. Full-width sections for impactful storytelling and a sticky header for consistent navigation. Maximize readability and visual hierarchy on all devices.
- Subtle, deliberate animations will enhance user interaction, including smooth transitions for theme switching, graceful hover effects on interactive elements, and a distinct fade-in/zoom-in effect for modals. A gentle pulse animation on the floating WhatsApp button will draw attention without distraction.
